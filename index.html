WELCOME TO CHECKERS!

OBJECTIVE:

The objective of this game is to capture all of your opponents checkers or cross them to the other side.

THE RULES OF THE GAME:

  • Move checker piece one space diagonally. Piece cannot be moved backwards until becomes a KING.
  • If opponent's checker piece is on a forward diagonal square and the square after is empty, jump your checker piece over the opponent's checker piece.
  • KING checker pieces may go back across the checkerboard.
  • Once your checker piece reaches the opposite side of board, it is crowned as a KING.
